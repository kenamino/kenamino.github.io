02 About

I build tools that feel alive.

I'm drawn to the intersection of engineering and aesthetics — where functional code meets intentional design. Most of my work lives in the terminal or on the desktop, where I can control every pixel and every frame.

My projects share a common thread: the "void" aesthetic. Dark surfaces, organic textures, systems that breathe and glitch. I believe software should have personality — not just features.
